Background Information


Rice Plant RicePlantDoctor is an interactive computer game that simulates the experience of a Filipino small-holder rice farmer as he attempts to earn a profit producing rice. The game was initially developed by a geographer, Robert Huke1, and modified with Philippine data by Carol Ferguson2, an Agricultural Economics graduate student, to illustrate the many problems and risks encountered by small-holders trying to utilize the new technologies of the Green Revolution. It was designed for use on a main-frame computer.
